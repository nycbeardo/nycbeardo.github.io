About


CURIOUS, PRACTICAL, THOUGHTFUL


I am a hybrid designer-developer based in New York City. My passions include Digital Art, Human-Computer Interaction, accessability, and tech/design ed for underrepresented groups.

I believe in advocating for good design that is easy to use, provides a enjoyable experience and most importantly has good social impact for society at large.

In addition I believe in promoting new methodologies as well as understanding one another and fair collaboration is crucial when it comes to the products and services we build. Thoughtful design and equality in tech and design are extremely important in today's ever connected world.

When I'm not creating, on my downtime you can find me engaged with others on social media, going to meetups, or looking for the next great thing to eat and write about on my food blog
