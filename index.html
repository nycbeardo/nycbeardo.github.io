About Me

I am a Web Developer and aspiring User Interface designer from New York City. I enjoy everything involving Web Development, User Interaction Desgin, Human-Computer Interaction, and the Internet of Things. My passion for design and technology started from my youth and transitioned into me earning a degree in Computer Information Systems and currently in pursuit of another while independently learning about various web technologies. I been naviagting the world of web development for the past 3 years, grasping many concepts and honing my skillset along the way.

In addition to spending my time working with Front End Web development, I also work with Javascript, and have experience with React, and Node. I've dealt with frameworks such as Bootstrap, Foundation & Semantic. I am currently diving into the world of Python and Ruby. On my downtime, you can find me hanging out on twitter, showing my photography chops on Instagram, or giving back by volunteering my time to various organiztaions across New York. I am also a avid foodie and regularly take pictures for my Instagram as well as for my WordPress.
